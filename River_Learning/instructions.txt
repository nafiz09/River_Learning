Solve the puzzle on the logs to make them disappear.
Logs have 3 types of puzzles on them:
MATHEMATICAL: Type the answer of the expression from  the keyboard and press enter.
SHAPES: Click on the buttons(left side of the screen) of the correct shape in the same sequence as the namesappears on the log.
COLORS: Click on the buttons(right side of the screen)of the correct color in the same sequence as the namesappears on the log.
